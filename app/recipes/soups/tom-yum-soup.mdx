title: Tom Yum soup
---

1. Chop & saute
   1. <Ing name="Lemongrass" quantity={2} unit="stalk"/>, tough outer bits removed, roughly chopped
   2. <Ing name="Garlic" quantity={3} unit="clove"/>, crushed
   3. <Ing name="Chillies" quantity={4} />
   4. <Ing name="Kaffir lime leaves" quantity={6} />, as is
2. Cook on high with 500ml 2 chicken stocks & <Ing name="Galangal paste" quantity={90} unit="g"/> for 15 minutes then blend
3. Cook for 15 mins, pulse occasionally?
   1. <Ing name="Coriander" quantity={4} unit="sprig"/> stalks and smaller leaves
   2. <Ing name="Grape tomatoes" quantity={100} unit="g"/>, halved
   3. <Ing name="Fish sauce" quantity={2} unit="tbsp"/>
   4. <Ing name="Light brown sugar" quantity={1} unit="tbsp"/>
   5. <Ing name="Mushroom" quantity={200} unit="g"/>
   6. <Ing name="Chicken" quantity={300} unit="g"/>
   7. <Ing name="Chicken stock" quantity={2} unit="pot" />
   8. Top up to line
4. Season with <Ing name="Lime juice" quantity={1} />, rest of coriander, maybe pulse
