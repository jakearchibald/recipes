title: Curry noodle soup
---

1. Chop and saute
   1. Roughly cut the <Ing name="Onion" quantity={1} /> & chuck it in the thing
   2. Put the <Ing name="Chillies" quantity={3} /> in if you're adding them
   3. A dash of oil
   4. <Ing name="Pepper" />
   5. I wouldn't bother adding salt/MSG, there's usually enough in the curry sauce
   6. A little <Ing name="Dry thyme"/>
2. Chunky cook
   1. Add <Ing name="Chicken" quantity={255} unit="g"/> (raw is fine), cut into thinish strips
   2. Add <Ing name="Chinese curry blocks" quantity={4} unit="serving"/>
   3. Add <Ing name="Carrot" quantity={160} unit="g"/>, cut into thin circles
   4. Fill with boiling water to the hot-max line
3. 6 mins from the end, add the <Ing name="Dry egg noodles" quantity={45} unit="g"/>
4. A quick pulse or two once it's done, if the noodles need broken up a bit
