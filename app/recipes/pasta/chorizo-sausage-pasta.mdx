---
title: Chorizo & sausage pasta
---

# Prepare

1. Dice <Ing name="Onion" quantity={1} />.
1. Mince <Ing name="Garlic" quantity={1} unit="clove" />.
1. Slice <Ing name="Chorizo" quantity={75} unit="g" />.
1. Squeeze <Ing name="Pork sausage" quantity={1.5} /> out of their skins.
1. Boil <Ing name="Pasta" quantity={200} unit="g" /> (rigatoni or penne) in salted water until al dente. Reserve a splash of the starchy pasta water before draining.

# Cook

1. **Crisp the meat:** Fry the chorizo and crumbled sausage in a large pan over medium heat until crisp and the oil has rendered. Remove from the pan and set aside.
1. **Soften the base:** In the leftover meat oil, soften the onion and garlic for a few minutes. Stir in <Ing name="Smoked paprika" quantity={0.5} unit="tsp" />.
1. **Build the sauce:** Add <Ing name="Passata" quantity={200} unit="g" /> and the meat. Simmer for 8 minutes.
1. **Finish:** Stir in <Ing name="Double cream" quantity={75} unit="ml" />, toss with the pasta (add a splash of pasta water if too thick), and garnish with <Ing name="Fresh basil" quantity={1} unit="pack" />.
