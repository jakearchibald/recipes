---
title: Simple chicken & mushroom pasta
---

# Prepare

1. Dice <Ing name="Onion" quantity={1} />.
1. Into one bowl:
   - Slice <Ing name="Mushroom" quantity={200} unit="g"/>.
   - Mince <Ing name="Garlic" quantity={3} unit="clove"/>.
   - Chop <Ing name="Chillies" quantity={3} />.
1. Boil <Ing name="Pasta" quantity={200} unit="g"/> in salted water according to the standard instructions until al dente. Reserve a splash of the starchy pasta water before draining.
1. Create 100ml <Ing name="Chicken stock" quantity={1} unit='pot' />.
1. Cut <Ing name="Chicken" quantity={2} unit="breast"/> into bite-sized pieces and rub in <Ing name="Cajun seasoning"/> & <Ing name="Pepper" />.

# Cook

1. **Sear the Chicken:** Heat olive oil and <Ing name="Butter"/> in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Fry chicken until golden and cooked through (about 5–6 minutes). Remove from the pan and set aside.
1. **Sauté the Veg:** In the same pan, cook the onion for 2 minutes.
1. Add mushrooms, garlic, and chillies, sautéing until the mushrooms are soft and browned (approx. 6 minutes).
1. **Build the Sauce:** Stir in <Ing name="Mustard powder" quantity={0.5} unit="tsp"/> and pour in chicken stock (and wine, if using). Let it simmer for 3 minutes.
1. Reduce the heat and stir in <Ing name="Double cream" quantity={150} unit="ml"/> & <Ing name="MSG" quantity={0.5} unit="tsp"/>, letting it bubble gently for 5 minutes until thickened.
1. **Combine:** Toss the cooked pasta and chicken into the sauce. If it's too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water. Season to taste.
1. Serve with sprinkle of <Ing name="Grated parmesan"/>.
