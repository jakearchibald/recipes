---
title: Simple chicken & mushroom pasta
---

1. **Rub chicken** in <Ing name="Cajun seasoning"/>
2. **Sear the Chicken:** Heat olive oil and <Ing name="Butter"/> in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Season <Ing name="Chicken" quantity={2} unit="breast"/> with <Ing name="Pepper"/>, then fry until golden and cooked through (about 5–6 minutes). Remove from the pan and set aside.
3. **Sauté the Veg:** In the same pan, cook <Ing name="Onion" quantity={1} /> for 2 minutes. Add <Ing name="Mushroom" quantity={200} unit="g"/>, <Ing name="Garlic" quantity={3} unit="clove"/>, and <Ing name="Chillies" quantity={3} />, sautéing until the mushrooms are soft and browned (approx. 6 minutes).
4. **Build the Sauce:** Stir in <Ing name="Mustard powder" quantity={0.5} unit="tsp"/> and pour in 100ml <Ing name="Chicken stock" quantity={1} unit='pot' /> (and wine, if using). Let it simmer for 3 minutes. Reduce the heat and stir in <Ing name="Double cream" quantity={150} unit="ml"/> & <Ing name="MSG" quantity={0.5} unit="tsp"/>, letting it bubble gently for 5 minutes until thickened. Maybe add more cajun.
5. **Cook the Pasta:** While the sauce simmers, boil <Ing name="Pasta" quantity={200} unit="g"/> in salted water according to the standard instructions until al dente. Reserve a splash of the starchy pasta water before draining.
6. **Combine:** Toss the cooked pasta and chicken into the sauce. If it's too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water. Season to taste.
7. Serve with sprinkle of <Ing name="Grated parmesan"/>
