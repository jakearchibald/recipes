---
title: Amaretto sour
---

Serves 1.

1. In the shaker:
   - <Ing name="Amaretto" quantity={150} unit="ml"/>
   - <Ing name="Bourbon" quantity={25} unit="ml"/>
   - <Ing name="Lemon juice" quantity={27} unit="ml"/>
   - <Ing name="Simple syrup" quantity={1} unit="tsp"/>
   - <Ing name="Egg white" quantity={15} unit="ml"/>
1. Shake ~15 seconds.
1. Add ice, shake until cold
1. Strain into glass with ice.
