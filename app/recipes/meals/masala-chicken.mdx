---
title: Masala Chicken
---

Based on [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H0UR31mFQjQ).

Serve with either rice and/or naan.

# Prepare

1. Spice mix (heaped tsp):
   - <Ing name="Garam Masala" quantity={1} unit="tsp" />
   - <Ing name="Coriander powder" quantity={2} unit="tsp" />
   - <Ing name="Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder" quantity={2} unit="tsp" />
   - <Ing name="Cumin powder" quantity={1} unit="tsp" />
   - <Ing name="Turmeric powder" quantity={0.25} unit="tsp" />
   - <Ing name="MSG" quantity={1} unit="tsp" /> (or salt)
1. Dice <Ing name="White onion" quantity={1} />.
1. Chop <Ing name="Green chillies" quantity={3} /> (large). Don't slice too thinly.
1. Cut <Ing name="Chicken" quantity={500} unit="g" /> into small chunks.
1. Finely chop <Ing name="Coriander" quantity={15} unit="sprig" />, including stems.

# Cook

1. In a coverable pan, add <Ing name="Butter" quantity={75} unit="g" />. Medium heat.
1. When butter is melted, add onions and combine. Cover for about 10 minutes. Quick stir every now & then.
1. Once onions are golden, add <Ing name="Ginger & garlic paste" quantity={1} unit="tbsp" />
1. Mix. Once golden, add:
   - <Ing name="Passata" quantity={4} unit="tbsp" />
   - Spice mix.
1. Cook for a couple of minutes uncovered, stirring frequently. The oil should start to separate.
1. Stir in <Ing name="Yogurt (full fat)" quantity={2} unit="tbsp" />.
1. Add chicken & stir until chicken is coated & sealed.
1. Add chillies.
1. Lid on, simmer for 5 minutes.
1. Uncover, allow any excess liquid to evaporate.
1. Add up to <Ing name="Water" quantity={200} unit="ml" /> if its looking dry.
1. Simmer on high for 1 minute.
1. Add coriander.
1. Stir and serve.
