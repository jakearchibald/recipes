1. Chop & saute
   1. Lemongrass, tough outer bits removed, roughly chopped
   2. Garlic, crushed
   3. Chili
   4. Lime leaves, as is
2. Cook on high with 500ml 2 chicken stocks & galangal for 15 minutes then blend
3. Cook for 15 mins, pulse occasionally?
   1. Coriander stalks and smaller leaves
   2. Tomatoes, halved
   3. Fish sauce
   4. Sugar
   5. Mushrooms
   6. Chicken
   7. Top up to line
4. Season with lime, rest of coriander, maybe pulse
