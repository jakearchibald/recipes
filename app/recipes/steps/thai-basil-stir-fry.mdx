---
title: Thai basil stir fry
---

1. Mix sauce ingredients in jug
   - <Ing name="Oyster sauce" quantity={2} unit="tsp"/>
   - <Ing name="Soy sauce" quantity={2} unit="tsp"/>
   - <Ing name="Sugar" quantity={1} unit="tsp"/>
   - <Ing name="Water" quantity={2} unit="tbsp"/>
1. Heat oil, add <Ing name="Garlic" quantity={2} unit="clove"/> and <Ing name="Chillies" quantity={3} />, cook for 10 seconds
1. Add <Ing name="Spring onion" quantity={1} /> and <Ing name="Chicken" quantity={225} unit="g"/> and cook for a couple of minutes
1. Add sauce
1. Add <Ing name="Thai basil" quantity={1} unit="pack"/> leaves and cook until wilted
1. Serve on <Ing name="Jasmine rice" quantity={2} unit="serving"/>
